Akhila Bharatha Khatik Samaj is planning to construct function hall near the temple premises for the benefit of Khatika Samaj community members. The funtion hall shall have a dining hall, kitchen room, guest rooms, bath rooms and many other facilities.
Requesting Samaj members to kindly donate towards this noble cause




Sri Sunama Jakini Matha was born into Suryavamshi Are-Katika community of Pinnepalli village in Anantapur district, Andhra Pradesh, India. A temple was built for this goddess near Gooty town in Anantapur district is also one of the first temple for Are-katika community.

Sri Sunama Jakini matha was born in Malkari family and married to Hanumanthakari family.

The foundation stone for temple was laid on 22-02-2002 and prana pratishtha was done on 14-02-2003. On second annual celebration i.e., on 17-02-2005 and 18-02-2005 Shikhara was consecrated. From that year on every year annual celebrations are conducted on second friday of Magha (month).
